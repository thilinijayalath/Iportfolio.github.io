What is Grooming in Professional Life?

Grooming in Professional Life is a regular act which professionals work on consistently. It is a process of caring and improving your own appearances. Self-grooming is thought to be far too vital for career development which involves dressing and even personal hygiene. We love to hang out with people who know how to dress and behave, the same goes for a workplace. When an employee or colleague displays a rude attitude, people tend to drift away. Management chose to neglect such staff during the promotion process.



The Elements of Professionalism




Professional Standards in the Workplace

It is also highly important to maintain professional appearance and image at all times. Proper grooming and professional appearance can certainly make you stand out and be ahead of the competition.

  1. Wear business suits in basic colors
  2. Always be neat and clean including your teeth, fingernails, face, hair and even your shoes
  3. Keep your pockets empty and as much as possible avoid tinkling coins or keys and bulges
  4. Avoid eating candies, smoking cigarettes and chewing gum when you are inside the office
  5. Use portfolio case or light briefcase when carrying important documents with you instead of compiling these documents in folders and carrying these between your armpits
  6. As much as possible get rid of tattoos and body piercings for these will just make you look untidy and unprofessional
  7. Wear light perfume and cologne and minimize using lots of jewelries


Dress Codes for Men and Women

Dress codes in business are based on the assumption that the perceptions of the public are most important. There are typically four types of corporate dress codes: business formal, business professional, business casual, and casual.

People can determine by looking at your clothing things like your economic status, social status and degree of professionalism. Most of all what you choose to wear tells people how you feel about yourself and can have an affect on your moods and behaviour.




Grooming Tips for Every Professional


  1. Personal hygiene

    If you are not clean and smell no one will want to be near you. Ensure body odour is fresh, clean, and pleasant. Reduce body odour by: regularly cleansing, especially with medicated cleansers, use deodorants, detoxify the body, avoid synthetic fabrics, avoid tight fitting clothes

  2. Health

    Maintain healthy teeth & fresh breath by: brushing twice a day, using a soft brush, floss regularly, get your teeth cleaned and checked twice a year by a dentist, drink lots of water to help wash away bacteria and residue

  3. Skin

    Skin is clear and healthy. Twice a day: cleanse, rinse, tone, moisturize

  4. Hair

    Hair is clean and current with a flattering cut. If coloured, it is a flattering shade, without obvious re-growth. Any facial hair is clean and neat. Eyebrows are groomed, nose hairs are not apparent

  5. Nails

    Nails are clean, trimmed, and in good condition. Nail polish is not chipped. Manicures should be ideally done once every 1 to 2 weeks, and a pedicure once every 3 to 4 weeks. Manicures and pedicures can be done professionally in a salon, or at home

  6. Fragrance

    If fragrance is worn, it is subtle. Fragrance use has become a sensitive subject over the last decade. As many people are allergic, caution needs to be exercised as to when to indulge Generally, it’s best to avoid wearing fragrance in business situations. When you do wear a scent, do not over-indulge! You may not be able to detect the fragrance on you, but others can

  7. Makeup

    If makeup is worn, it is applied sparingly and properly, and not smeared or caked. Wear colours that complement you

  8. Footwear

    Footwear is clean, polished, and in good condition

  9. Eyewear

    If you wear it is clean and in good condition

  10. Clothing

    Clothing is clean and in good condition; buttons are sewn on tightly, hems are in place, and fabric is not pilling. Ensure that clothing fits properly; there are no drag lines from being too tight, or bagging from being too big. Underwear is not visible.
